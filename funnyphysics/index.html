Movement of Objects.

We appreciate the wonders of Physics that happen every day around us.

DISCOVER WHITH US

Where you will find mainly movements of objects in our environment.

Every day can be a wonderful adventure, if you stop for a moment and look around you at the various physical phenomena that are occurring at the macroscopic level, even if you think about it more carefully we can notice those at the microscopic or quantum level.

Our Home

When watching television, listening to music or even taking a shower, we can notice the related physical phenomena.

Our Ward

Believe it or not, for example, if you think that we are at rest, the earth moves at a speed of 1600 kilometers per hour.

In the Street

You can see cars, motobikes, bikes or people, and other objects moving because of a force applied to them.

In Our Job

From the air conditioning to the lighting that we have, many physical entities can be observed in the work environment that will always be there even if we do not notice them.

In Our Study

The letters of the printed texts, or the electronic devices do not show another facet of the physics in our daily life.

The great thing about seeing movements on a large scale and at the microscopic level.

Light behaves as a wave and as a particle due to its dual characteristics, which is why it is called a wavelet.

The sweat of our body when carrying out any physical activity: it is only the regulation of the heat that comes out in order to bring the body temperature to acceptable levels.

The light from other planets or stars is moved due to the gravitational mass of other celestial bodies, called gravitational lensing.
